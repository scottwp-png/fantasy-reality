FAQ
Got questions?
It's like fantasy football, but for reality TV. You draft contestants from shows like Survivor, Top Chef, and Love Island, earn points based on what happens each episode, and compete against your friends all season long.
Yes, completely free to play. Create a league, invite friends, and start drafting.
Sign up, tap "New League" on the home screen, pick your show, and name your league. Then generate invite codes for your friends from the Teams tab.
Get an invite code from your league's commissioner. You can enter it when you sign up (on the "Join League" tab) or after logging in on the home screen.
Multiple formats including Heroes (one-time draft with Hero 2x and Side-Kick 1.5x multipliers, one swap per week), Standard (weekly snake redraft), and more formats coming soon like Survivor Pool, Predictions, and Duo Draft.
Your league's commissioner scores each episode. They pick which events happened (challenge wins, eliminations, drama moments, etc.) and assign them to contestants. Points are fully customizable — your league decides what's worth what.
The commissioner runs the league — they score episodes, manage settings, generate invite codes, and lock rosters before episodes air. Whoever creates a league is automatically the commissioner.
We have pre-built templates for Survivor, Top Chef, Love Island, The Bachelor/ette, Great British Bake Off, The Traitors, Big Brother, The Challenge, RuPaul's Drag Race, and The Amazing Race. You can also create a custom league for any show.
The site works as a Progressive Web App. On your phone, open the site in your browser, then use "Add to Home Screen" to install it. It looks and feels like a native app.
