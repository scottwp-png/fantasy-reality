Features
All the strategy of fantasy sports.
None of the sports.

Custom Scoring

Dramatic exit? 10 points. Ugly cry? 1 point. Your league, your rules. Every show gets a starter rulebook and your commissioner can override per-league.

Live Standings

Rankings update the moment scores save. Weekly breakdowns, best weeks, worst weeks, head-to-head records — all the receipts.

Multiple Formats

Heroes (one-time draft, 2x/1.5x multipliers), Standard (weekly redraft), Survivor Pool, Salary Cap, Elimination Pool, Predictions. Match the format to your group.

Auto Roster Lock

Rosters lock before the episode airs so no one swaps in last-minute. Each show has its own air-time schedule that commissioners can override.

Co-Commissioners

Share the workload. Promote any team owner to co-commissioner — they can score, lock rosters, manage cast, and finalize weeks.

Spoiler Protection

Scores stay blurred until you watch. Reveal when you're ready, or auto-reveal after a grace period your league sets.

Cast Rankings

See who's dominating the season, who's quietly stacking points, and who your league is sleeping on.

Works Everywhere

Phone, tablet, laptop. Install it like an app or just use your browser — same experience either way.

FAQ
Got questions?
It's like fantasy football, but for reality TV. You draft contestants from shows like Survivor, Top Chef, and Love Island, earn points based on what happens each episode, and compete against your friends all season long.
Yes, completely free to play. Create a league, invite friends, and start drafting.
Sign up, tap "New League" on the home screen, pick your show, and name your league. Then generate invite codes for your friends from the Teams tab.
Get an invite code from your league's commissioner. You can enter it when you sign up (on the "Join League" tab) or after logging in on the home screen.
Six formats, all live: Heroes (one-time draft, Hero 2× / Side-Kick 1.5× / Vigilante, one swap per week), Standard (weekly snake redraft, points pile up), Best Ball (auto-optimized lineup from your full roster), Salary Cap (fixed budget, priced contestants), Survivor Pool (one pick, last one standing wins), and Elimination Pool (weekly pick, can't reuse).
Each league's commissioner scores every episode — tap a rule, tap which contestants it applied to, save. Rules and point values are fully customizable per league. For big shows, an admin can score globally and that scoring cascades into every opted-in league, then each commissioner can adjust per their own judgment.
The commissioner runs the league — scores episodes, manages settings, generates invite codes, locks rosters before episodes air, and finalizes weeks. Whoever creates a league is automatically the commissioner, and they can promote any team owner to co-commissioner to share the workload.
When you watch on delay, you don't want to open the app and see scores that reveal what happened. Spoiler protection blurs scoring for the current week until you choose to reveal — or auto-reveals after a grace period your league sets. Each member controls when they peek.
Pre-built templates for Survivor, Top Chef, Love Island, The Bachelor/ette, Great British Bake Off, The Traitors, Big Brother, The Challenge, RuPaul's Drag Race, The Amazing Race, and Love is Blind. You can also create a custom league with your own scoring rules for any show.
The site works as a Progressive Web App. On your phone, open it in your browser, then use "Add to Home Screen" to install it. Looks and feels like a native app, no app store needed.
