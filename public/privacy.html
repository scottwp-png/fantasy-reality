Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Fantasy Reality TV collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

1 Who This Policy Applies To

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of app.fantasyrealitytv.com and fantasyrealitytv.com (collectively, "Fantasy Reality TV," "FRTV," "we," "us," or "our").

Fantasy Reality TV is intended for users who are 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with their information, please contact us at admin@fantasyrealitytv.com.

2 Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Category What We Collect How
Account Information Email address, display name Provided by you at registration or via Google Sign-In
League & Game Data Leagues you create or join, team names, roster choices, scores Generated by your use of the platform
User-Submitted Content League names, contestant information, photos uploaded by commissioners Provided by you
Usage Data App interactions, feature usage, error logs Collected automatically via Firebase
Device & Technical Data Browser type, operating system, IP address Collected automatically by Firebase and (in future) advertising partners

We do not collect payment information. Fantasy Reality TV is currently free to use and does not process financial transactions.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Operate the fantasy league platform and display your team and score data
  • Allow commissioners to manage leagues and invite other users
  • Communicate with you about your account or the service
  • Monitor and improve platform performance and fix bugs
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Display advertising (if and when introduced — see Section 7)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4 Firebase & Google Services

Fantasy Reality TV is built on Firebase, a platform provided by Google LLC. Firebase handles authentication (sign-in), real-time data storage, and hosting infrastructure. By using FRTV, you acknowledge that your data is processed by Firebase in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

If you choose to sign in with Google, Google's authentication service will share your name, email address, and profile photo with us. We do not receive your Google password.

5 Data Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other league members: Your display name and team data are visible to other members of any league you join. This is core to how the platform works.
  • With service providers: Firebase/Google processes data on our behalf to operate the platform.
  • For legal compliance: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users or the public.
  • In a business transfer: If Fantasy Reality TV is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify users of any such change.

6 Data Retention

We retain your account and league data for as long as your account is active or as needed to operate the platform. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information from our active systems within a reasonable time, subject to legal retention requirements.

League data created by commissioners (contestant info, scores, etc.) may persist in the platform even after individual users depart, as it forms part of the shared league record.

7 Advertising

We do not currently serve advertisements. In the future, we may display ads through Google AdSense or similar platforms. If we do:

  • Google may use cookies and device identifiers to serve interest-based ads based on your browsing activity across sites.
  • You can opt out of interest-based advertising via Google's Ad Settings or the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool.
  • This Privacy Policy will be updated before advertising goes live.

8 Cookies & Tracking

Firebase may set cookies or use local storage to maintain your authentication session. We do not use additional tracking cookies at this time. If advertising is introduced, third-party ad cookies will be disclosed and addressed per applicable law.

9 Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request that inaccurate data be corrected
  • Deletion: Request that your personal data be deleted
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at admin@fantasyrealitytv.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10 Security

We use Firebase's built-in security infrastructure to protect your data, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS) and Firebase Authentication for account access. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

11 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the updated policy with a new effective date. For significant changes — particularly those relating to advertising or new data uses — we will make reasonable efforts to notify active users.
